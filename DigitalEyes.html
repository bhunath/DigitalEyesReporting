  • What is Digital Eyes?
    Digital Eye is technology enabled solution to take care of most precious human sense, Eyes! This Application is your partner in eye care and provides real time alerts to help you take correction actions.
  • Who is it for?
    Everyone who is using Digital Display.
  • Why is it needed?
    With technology disruption, digital screen time on laptops, tablets & mobile phones has significantly increased across all age groups & geographies. While working on Digital Screen we generally forget about time and up spending long hours without realizing the damage that we have done to our eyes. Once user is exposed to screen, it monitors and records important parameters like exposure to screen, Blink Rate, Distance between display device and user, Eye Redness and User touching face (Covid -19). The Application tracks and provides regular alerts to help user take corrective actions. The application generates reports and user trends, which can suggest user for potential Eye related problem.
  • What are the benefits of using this application?
    • Continuous Eye Health Monitoring

    • Providing alerts to user based on customized settings
    • Generating reports and trends, Suggesting user of potential Eye Related problem
